## Problem / Context

The client needed a modern studio website that does more than look good. The site had to:

- communicate premium positioning,
- attract local leads from Gdańsk, Gdynia, and Sopot,
- handle Polish and English content without UX friction,
- provide transparent trust signals (privacy, company data, terms),
- and be technically sound for Google and AI assistants.

The previous state had content and branding potential, but required a structured implementation focused on measurable outcomes.

## Approach

I treated this as a performance + SEO product build, not just a visual redesign:

1. Defined page architecture around intent (services, portfolio, process, contact, local landing pages).
2. Built a clean bilingual front-end in Astro with stable layout and clear CTA hierarchy.
3. Implemented technical SEO foundations first (canonical, hreflang, sitemap, robots, Open Graph, JSON-LD).
4. Iterated using audit feedback until key weak points were solved (AI crawler access, freshness signals, legal/trust links, mobile loading path).

## What I shipped

- Full static Astro website with production-ready deployment flow.
- Bilingual content switching (PL/EN) and localized key sections.
- Conversion-focused homepage with service framing, project previews, FAQ, and direct contact actions.
- Contact flow integrated through Formspree with dedicated thank-you page.
- Legal and trust pages integrated into navigation/footer (`Privacy`, `Terms`).
- Rich structured data graph and content discoverability files (`sitemap`, `robots`, `llms.txt`).

## Technical implementation details

### SEO and indexing

- Canonical URLs and hreflang handling.
- XML sitemap generation and robots directives.
- Open Graph and Twitter card metadata.
- Structured data for business + page context.

### GEO/AEO and AI visibility

- `llms.txt` published with key pages and business context.
- AI crawler access verified (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot).
- Freshness signals added (`article:modified_time`, `dateModified` in structured data).
- Added answer-friendly content patterns (lists, definitions, compact comparison table).

### Performance

- Prioritized hero loading path and controlled lazy-loading for non-critical assets.
- Reduced render-blocking behavior on homepage interactions.
- Kept static-first architecture to minimize runtime overhead.

### Compliance and trust

- Privacy policy and terms available and linked globally.
- Contact details visible and machine-readable.
- Local hosting/font/privacy choices aligned with GDPR-oriented implementation.

### Hosting and transport security

- **HSTS** (`Strict-Transport-Security`) is configured at the **Cloudflare** edge (SSL/TLS → Edge Certificates), not inside the Astro bundle — typical for static sites behind Cloudflare.
